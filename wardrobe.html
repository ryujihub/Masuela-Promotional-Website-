Waffle T Shirt

A waffle T-shirt is a casual and comfortable shirt featuring a waffle-like textured fabric, providing a laid-back yet stylish look and a unique, breathable texture.

Wide Leg Pants

Wide-leg pants are a fashionable and comfortable bottom wear option characterized by a roomy, loose-fitting silhouette that widens from the hips down. These pants offer a relaxed and versatile style suitable for various occasions.

Skirt

A skirt is a versatile bottom garment worn around the waist, available in various lengths and styles for diverse fashion choices.

Boots

Boots are a type of footwear that covers the foot and extends up the leg. They come in various styles, from ankle boots to knee-high or over-the-knee boots, and are known for their versatility, providing both fashion and functionality for different occasions and seasons.

Rubber Shoes

Korean rubber shoes are lightweight, comfortable footwear made of rubber, ideal for casual or water-related activities.
